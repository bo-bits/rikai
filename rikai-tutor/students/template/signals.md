# Signals

_Extracted interest and learning signals. Most recent at the top. Each entry links to the session it came from._

_Format: [YYYY-MM-DD] **Signal type** — what was observed, and why it's significant._

## Signal types
- **reaches-for** — unprompted connection or question toward something not in the lesson
- **avoids** — consistently skips, deflects, or shows low energy around a topic or style
- **lights-up** — strong engagement: follow-up questions, enthusiasm, goes beyond the prompt
- **confused-by** — recurring sticking point across sessions
- **explains-well** — can teach it back clearly; likely solid or mastered
